## HOW TO STUDY A SITE

1. **Open the site** in Chrome
2. **Open DevTools** (F12)
3. **Check Performance tab** — see what's animating
4. **Check Elements tab** — see CSS properties
5. **Check Console** — see any errors or logs
6. **Scroll slowly** — study how animations trigger
7. **Resize window** — study responsive behavior
8. **Test on mobile** — study touch interactions
9. **Note timing functions** — cubic-bezier values, duration
10. **Note color values** — hex, rgb, oklch

**Don't just look. Analyze.**

## THE 15 WEAPONS

### 1. Custom WebGL/WebGPU
- **What:** Full GPU access for 3D rendering
- **Example:** Oryzo.io (fluid simulation), IVRESS (WebGPU)
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** Renders millions of particles at 60fps

### 2. Real-time Physics
- **What:** Gravity, springs, collisions, cloth simulation
- **Example:** Bruno Simon (interactive 3D portfolio)
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** Elements feel physical, not digital

### 3. Scroll-driven 3D Camera
- **What:** Camera moves through 3D space on scroll
- **Example:** Hubtown (3D architectural visualization)
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** Feels like flying through a scene

### 4. Audio-reactive Visuals
- **What:** Visuals react to sound in real-time
- **Example:** ShipBridge hero (audio-reactive background)
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** Site feels alive with sound

### 5. Procedural Generation
- **What:** Unique visuals every visit
- **Example:** Generative art backgrounds
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** No two visits are the same

### 6. Custom Shaders
- **What:** GPU-programmed visual effects
- **Example:** Patrick Hurlburt (shader distortion)
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** Visual effects are unique and custom

### 7. Variable Font Animation
- **What:** Animate font-weight, width on scroll
- **Example:** Build Studio (kinetic typography)
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** Typography becomes the animation

### 8. GPGPU Particles
- **What:** 100K+ particles on GPU
- **Example:** Sleep Well Creative (particle system)
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** Large-scale particle effects

### 9. CSS Scroll-driven Animations
- **What:** CSS-only scroll animations (no JS)

### 10. View Transitions API

### 11. Container Queries
- **What:** Component-level responsive

### 12. Haptic Feedback
- **What:** Vibration on mobile interactions

- **Why:** Tactile feel on mobile

### 13. Device Motion Parallax
- **What:** Gyroscope-driven depth
- **Example:** Mobile 3D effects
- **Figma:** Impossible
- **Why:** Sensor-aware depth

### 14. Custom Cursor
- **What:** Cursor changes per section
- **Example:** Ilya Kostin (cursor shape changes)
- **Figma:** Impossible

### 15. Spring Physics
